Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Camels

  1. Camels' ears are furry
  2. Camels can move easily across sand because their feet are designed specifically for that
  3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible

Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons have extraordinary navigation systems
  2. They're in flocks of 20-30 birds
  3. They can fly above and beyond 6000 feet

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  2. Their eyes are different to normal bears
  3. Bamboo is critical to their environment
